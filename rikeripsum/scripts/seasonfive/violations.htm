grab hold of an elusive memory.

KEIKO

KEIKO

yes...

better.

KEIKO

Geordi and Data walking along a corridor.

be recovered... why would it have
been forgotten?

GEORDI

record every second of every event
in your life...

DATA
That is correct.

GEORDI
And if you want to recall any one
of those seconds, you just access
the proper memory circuit.

DATA
My understanding of the Human
brain suggests that the process
is the same for you.
(MORE)

the proper RNA sequence.

GEORDI

it happened last week.

TARMIN

will be a vast storehouse of these
individual memories.
